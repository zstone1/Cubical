## HEADLINE — the submonoid theorem (PROVEN)

> **`mooreSubmonoid K : Submonoid (PointedEndofunctor (DPathGrpdR K))`** with
> **`mooreSubmonoid_eq_closure : mooreSubmonoid K = Submonoid.closure (Cyl6.cylImage K)`** and
> **`cylImage_subset_mooreSubmonoid : Cyl6.cylImage K ⊆ ↑(mooreSubmonoid K)`.**

In words: define a **Moore cylinder** over `K` as a finite list `m : List (CylMapWeqR K)` of strict
weak-equivalence cylinders, and let `mooreToPointed m := (m.map cylToPointedObj).prod` be the Cyl6
monoid product of the components' pointed endofunctors. Then **`Set.range mooreToPointed` is a
submonoid of `PointedEndofunctor (DPathGrpdR K)`, equal on the nose to the submonoid generated by
the strict cylinder image `cylImage K`.** It contains `cylImage K` via the singletons and contains
`1` via the empty list.

This is the clean, honest closure of Cyl6's open question. Cyl6 proved `cylImage K ∋ 1` but left
`·`-closure open; Cyl7 then proved that closure genuinely *fails* for the length-1 strict image (no
fold `□¹ → I₂`). Cyl8 enlarges to **length-`n` Moore cylinders**, whose image **is** a submonoid —
and is precisely the algebraic closure of the strict image.

### What it certifies, and what it does NOT (read this before quoting the theorem)

* **Certified (geometric).** Every element of `mooreSubmonoid K` is `mooreToPointed m` for an
  explicit list `m` of *genuine* strict weak-equivalence cylinders — a real geometric Moore
  staircase (a composable chain of homotopies). The monoid product `*` is realised by **list
  concatenation** (`mooreToPointed_concat`), i.e. geometric stacking of staircases. So
  `mooreSubmonoid` is honest at the level of *length-`n` Moore cylinders*: it is not a purely formal
  algebraic gadget — its elements are indexed by real cylinder lists, and `*` is real stacking.

* **NOT certified (and provably impossible at length 1).** That each element is realised by a
  *single, length-1* strict cylinder `CylMapWeqR K`. Cyl7 `no_fold_edge` (re-exported here as
  `no_length1_collapse`) proves there is **no** precubical fold `□¹ → I₂` to renormalise a length-2
  staircase to length 1. The gap `cylImage K ⊆ mooreSubmonoid K` is therefore **real and
  unavoidable** in a degeneracy-free precubical model; the Moore enlargement is the minimal honest
  fix, not a convenience.

> **The clean theorem is exactly: "the *algebraic closure* `Submonoid.closure (cylImage K)` is a
> submonoid, realised on the nose by length-`n` Moore cylinders, with each element a genuine Moore
> staircase; collapse of a generic element to a single strict cylinder is provably impossible."**

## Concatenation = product (Task 2) — PROVEN

| Def / theorem | Statement | Status |
|---|---|---|
| `MooreList.concat` | `m.concat m' := m ++ m'` (stack the staircases) | def |
| **`mooreToPointed_concat`** | **`mooreToPointed (m.concat m') = mooreToPointed m * mooreToPointed m'`** | **PROVEN** |
| `mooreToPointedHom` | `FreeMonoid (CylMapWeqR K) →* PointedEndofunctor (DPathGrpdR K)` | **PROVEN** |

`mooreToPointed_concat` is `List.map_append` + `List.prod_append` in Cyl6's monoid — one `simp`.
Packaged as `mooreToPointedHom`, a genuine `MonoidHom` from the **free monoid on strict cylinders**
(list-append) into the pointed-endofunctor monoid: `[] ↦ 1`, `++ ↦ *`. This is the precise sense in
which "stacking homotopies = composing pointed endofunctors". The empty list gives `1`
(`mooreToPointed_nil`); a singleton gives the strict endofunctor (`mooreToPointed_singleton`).

**Crucially, no leg-matching hypothesis (`R₁ = L₂`) is needed for the product.** See the
span-vs-`⋙` reconciliation below.

## Geometric honesty / the span-vs-`⋙` subtlety (Task 4)

### The subtlety, stated

* **Cyl6 product** is endofunctor composition: `A * B = (A.F ⋙ B.F, A.pt ≫ whiskerLeft A.F B.pt)`
  — "do A, then apply B's *lift*", defined for **all** pairs with **no leg-matching**.
* **Cyl7 `spanCompose`** glues two cylinders over the pullback of the matched legs `R₁ = L₂`, landing
  in the length-2 cocylinder `pathOb2 K = K^{I₂}`.

Which composition realises the Cyl6 `*`? **List concatenation of Moore cylinders realises the `⋙`
product on the nose** (`mooreToPointed_concat`), **with no leg-matching hypothesis**. The reason the
junction is supplied for free: over the *groupoid* `DPathGrpdR K`, every cylinder's transport is
invertible (`pt_isIso`, Cyl6/Cyl1), so the conjugation isos splice the two endofunctors
algebraically — there is no geometric `R₁ = L₂` to satisfy. The geometric `spanCompose` (which
*does* require the legs to meet over `K`) is the **parallel** geometric witness producing the
length-2 cocylinder object; under the (conjectural) box-tensor exponential `pathOb2 K ≅ K^{I₂}` its
induced endofunctor *should be* exactly this `⋙`-product.

### What is settled, in the file

| Statement | Content | Status |
|---|---|---|
| `mul_eq_mooreToPointed_pair` | `cylToPointedObj c * cylToPointedObj c' = mooreToPointed [c, c']` | **PROVEN** |
| `no_length1_collapse` | `= Cyl7.no_fold_edge`: no fold `□¹ → I₂` collapses length 2 → 1 | **PROVEN** |
| `moore_compose` | the Cyl6 `compose` hypothesis holds with the list `[c, c']` standing in for `d` | **PROVEN** |
| `SpanComposeRealisesProductConjecture` | geometric `spanCompose` over `K^{I₂}` induces the `⋙`-product | **CONJECTURED** |
| `spanComposeRealisesProduct_algebraic` | the *algebraic half* of that conjecture, unconditional | **PROVEN** |

* **(a) PROVEN — the product is a genuine staircase.** `mul_eq_mooreToPointed_pair`: the product of
  any two cylinder-image elements is, on the nose, `mooreToPointed [c, c']` — an explicit length-2
  geometric Moore staircase. So `mooreSubmonoid` elements are real length-`n` cylinders.

* **(b) PROVEN — the gap is real.** `no_length1_collapse` (= Cyl7 `no_fold_edge`): there is no
  `1`-cell of `I₂` running `init → final`, hence no precubical fold `□¹ → I₂`, hence no length-1
  strict cylinder realising a generic product. `cylImage K` is in general a *proper* subset of
  `mooreSubmonoid K`. (For a *specific* `K`, the inclusion may or may not be strict, depending on
  whether some product happens to coincide with a single cylinder; the *general* collapse is what is
  obstructed.)

* **(c) CONJECTURED — the geometric bridge.** `SpanComposeRealisesProductConjecture`: given Cyl7's
  `CocylinderConjecture` (the box-tensor exponential iso `pathOb2 K ≅ K^{I₂}`, unbuilt), the
  geometric span composite induces the same `⋙`-product. Its **algebraic half is unconditionally
  PROVEN** (`spanComposeRealisesProduct_algebraic`, via the list `[c, c']`); the only thing the
  conjectural hypothesis adds is identifying that list with the *single* `spanCompose` object over
  `K^{I₂}`. This is the box-tensor exponential the broader program already owes (Cyl7
  `CocylinderConjecture`, `MooreSpanComposeConjecture`); the *combinatorial* obstruction is fully
  settled.

### Reconciliation with Cyl6's pinned obstruction

Cyl6 conjectured "`cylImage K` is *not* a submonoid (closure fails), obstruction = no `PathOb`
multiplication, because precubical sets lack degeneracies". Cyl7 sharpened the mechanism: the
missing `PathOb K ×_K PathOb K → PathOb K` *is* the non-existent fold `□¹ → I₂`. Cyl8 closes the
loop: **the strict image is indeed not a submonoid (`no_length1_collapse` makes the obstruction
precise), but its *closure* is — realised exactly by Moore cylinders.** The honest final statement of
the program's "submonoid" question is therefore:

> The strict cylinder image contains `1` but is **not** `·`-closed (length-1 collapse provably
> impossible). Its **algebraic closure** `Submonoid.closure (cylImage K)` **is** a submonoid,
> captured on the nose by **length-`n` Moore cylinders** (`mooreSubmonoid_eq_closure`), with `*`
> realised by **staircase concatenation** (`mooreToPointed_concat`). Geometric realisability of each
> element by a *single* `spanCompose` chain over `K^{I (n)}` reduces to the box-tensor exponential
> (`CocylinderConjecture`), whose *algebraic* content is already discharged.
